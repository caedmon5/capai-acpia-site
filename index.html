Canadian Association for the Philosophy of AI

We are the Canadian Association for the Philosophy of Artificial Intelligence. Our association was established in 2025 by Martina Orlandi (Trent University) and Nicholas Dunn (University of Lethbridge).

Nous sommes l'Association canadienne pour la philosophie de l'intelligence artificielle. Notre association a été fondée en 2025 par Martina Orlandi (Trent University) et Nicholas Dunn (University of Lethbridge).

About CAPAI-ACPIA

CAPAI-ACPIA brings together researchers, scholars, and students across Canada who are working on philosophical questions raised by artificial intelligence. We host talks, workshops, and collaborative research on topics ranging from the ethics of machine learning to the foundations of computational cognition.

Whether you work in philosophy, computer science, law, or any field that intersects with AI — we welcome your participation.
